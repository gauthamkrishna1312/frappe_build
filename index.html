Frappe buildathon contest

Frappe is an open-source web framework that is used to develop enterprise-level applications. The IEDC AWH chapter has initiated a Frappe development contest that aims to provide a platform for participants to learn and develop their skills in Frappe within a week. The Technical Support is provided by efeone Academy.

Once you register the event you will be added to a telegram link and you can discuss about your project


The Contest

The contest is an excellent opportunity for students and developers who are interested in learning Frappe. By participating in the contest, they will be able to learn new skills and develop their expertise in Frappe. Moreover, they will be able to interact with other participants and learn from their experiences as well.The Frappe development contest initiated by IEDC AWH chapter is an excellent opportunity for participants to learn and develop their skills in Frappe. The contest runs for a week, and participants will be given tasks that they need to complete using Frappe. Technical support is provided by efeone Academy, which means that participants will be able to get help if they face any issues during the contest.
